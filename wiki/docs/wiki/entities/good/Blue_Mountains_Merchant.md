# Blue Mountains Merchant

Blue Mountains Merchants are the traveling traders [[blue_mountains_faction|Blue Mountains]]. They wander around Middle Earth seeking to trade their wares to players.

## Spawning

Blue Mountains Merchants can spawn anywhere on Middle-Earth aside from their homeland, evil-infested biome like Mordor, and desolate biomes. They will spawn with a detachment of [[blue_mountains_warrior|Blue Mountains Soldiers]].
  
## Behavior

Merchants randomly appear somewhere, wandering around with their guards looknig for someone to trade with them. After a single Minecraft day, they disappear off of the map. When Merchants are killed, a coin symbol floats above where they were killed and they will respawn.

## Equipment

Merchants are not fighting NPCs and will only attack if you attack them back. They wear no armour and only use a dagger in combat.
  
## Trading

Merchants will not trade with players until they are at least 5+ alignment with the [[blue_mountains_faction|Blue Mountains]]. They offer food, drinks, and lots of equipment and decor from their faction. They will purchase food, coal and ingots from players.

## Strategy

- Generally you do not need to attack a hiring unit like a merchant, but if you do, long weapons are generally more effective against melee NPCs, especially dwarves as they have shorter reach.
- Try to draw the merchant away from its guard, as a large group can overwhelm you very quickly.

## Lore

The Blue Mountains Merchants wandering the land, buying and selling precious materials to bring back to their homeland of Ered Luin.
