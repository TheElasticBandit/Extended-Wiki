# Imladris Guard

Imladris Guards are the protectors of the Last Homely House: Rivendell. They guard their Lord Elrond from any evil that would inavde their precious valley.

## Spawning

Imladris Guards can spawn any time of the day, only at the Last Homely House location.
  
## Behavior

Imladris Guards will attack any player or creature aligned with an evil faction, which due to their location, generally means [[gundabad_faction|Gundabad]] units. 
They generally wander around aimlessly when they are not fighting enemies.

## Equipment

Imladris Guards fight enemies with a Rivendell Bow and a random Rivendell melee weapon, which are only the sword and spear at the moment. Imladris Guards cannot be hired, but are a very strong unit due to their high tier armor.

## Strategy

- When fighting Imladris Guards or any other ranged enemy, a shield is extremely useful to block arrows.
- Fighting Elven Warriors is almost always a bad idea: They have a strong ranged attack and full armor, so if you want to fight them, you better bring an army.
- If you are fighting larger numbers, try to draw them towards you one by one, as a large group can overwhelm you with arrows very quickly.

## Lore

The Rivendell Elves are Noldorin Elves who dwell in Rivendell, home of the Lord Elrond. They are more ancient than the Wood-Elves and Galadhrim, and are great warriors and loremasters.
