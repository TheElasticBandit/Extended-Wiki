# Blue Mountains Dwarf

Blue Mountain Dwarves are a common sight across the Blue Mountains. They spawn randomly as well as in Blue Mountains Strongholds and Settlements.

## Behavior

When not wandering the mountainsides, dwarves will attack automatically when an enemy player or NPC enters their range.

## Equipment

Blue Mountains dwarves wear no armour, making them a decently easy NPC to kill. They will charge you unlike traders and attack with daggers.

## Hiring

They can be hired for 30 coins from [[blue_mountains_commander|Blue Mountains Commanders]] when one's aignment is >=200 with the [[blue_mountain_faction|Blue Mountains]].

## Strategy

- When fighting dwarves of any kind, a spear or other long ranged weapon is useful as dwaves have shorter range.
- If you are fighting larger numbers, try to draw them towards you one by one, as a large group can overwhelm you with arrows very quickly.

## Lore

The Blue Mountains are semi peaceful civilians, who wander their homeland of Ered Luin and attack enemies only when attacked.
