# Bree-land Potter

The Bree-land Potter is a trader that specializes in bricks and wautle and daub.

## Spawning

The Bree-land Potter spawns in the marketplaces of Bree-land villages.
  
## Behavior

Bree-land Potters will stand in their stalls when not interacting. Upon death, a silver coin will float above the ground where they died, signifying that they will respawn here after a short period of time.

## Equipment

Bree-land Potters fight with any of the following: Iron and bronze daggers and iron, bronze and stone axes.
  
## Trading

Potters will not trade with players if they have negative alignment with Bree-land. Upon trading with them, the player will be bestowed the advancement **"Insider Bargains"**.

Potters sell the goods of their trade. This includes bricks and blocks.

Bree-land Potters also buy many supplies, such as shovels and clay.

## Strategy

- There is no need to fight a Bree-land Potter, but if you do, it is easy to kill them with a longer weapon such as a spear, or with a bow, since they don't have a ranged attack, just watch out for their axes!

## Lore

The Bree-land people were men who wandered over the Misty Mountains but did not continue west like the Edain. They found the area to be pleasant, and settled there for good. They were there before the Númenóreans, after the kingdom of Arnor, and will be forever according to them.
