# Bree-land Cheesemonger

The Bree-land Cheesemonger is a trader that specializes in cheeses.

## Spawning

The Bree-land Cheesemonger spawns in the cheese-shop, found in Bree-land villages.
  
## Behavior

Bree-land Cheesemongers will wander around their shop when not selling goods. Upon death, a silver coin will float above the ground where they died, signifying that they will respawn here after a short period of time.

## Equipment

Bree-land Cheesemongers fight with any of the following: Iron and bronze daggers and iron, bronze and stone axes.
  
## Trading

Cheesemongers will not trade with players if they have negative alignment with Bree-land.

Cheesemongers sell the goods of their trade. This includes cheese and cheese curds.

Bree-land Cheesemongers also buy many supplies, such as knifes and mushrooms.

## Strategy

- There is no need to fight a Bree-land Cheesemonger, but if you do, it is easy to kill them with a longer weapon such as a spear, or with a bow, since they don't have a ranged attack, just watch out for their axes!

## Lore

The Bree-land people were men who wandered over the Misty Mountains but did not continue west like the Edain. They found the area to be pleasant, and settled there for good. They were there before the Númenóreans, after the kingdom of Arnor, and will be forever according to them.
