# Bree-land Stablemaster

The Bree-land Stablemaster is a trader that specializes in horses.

## Spawning

The Bree-land Stablemaster spawns in the stables, found in Bree-land villages.
  
## Behavior

Bree-land Stablemasters will wander around their stable when not selling goods. Upon death, a silver coin will float above the ground where they died, signifying that they will respawn here after a short period of time.

## Equipment

Bree-land Stablemasters fight with any of the following: Iron and bronze daggers and iron, bronze and stone axes.
  
## Trading

Stablemasters will not trade with players if they have negative alignment with Bree-land.

Stablemasters sell the goods of their trade. This includes staddles and horse armor.

Bree-land Stablemasters also buy many supplies, such as animal feed.

## Strategy

- There is no need to fight a Bree-land Stablemaster, but if you do, it is easy to kill them with a longer weapon such as a spear, or with a bow, since they don't have a ranged attack, just watch out for their axes!

## Lore

The Bree-land people were men who wandered over the Misty Mountains but did not continue west like the Edain. They found the area to be pleasant, and settled there for good. They were there before the Númenóreans, after the kingdom of Arnor, and will be forever according to them.
