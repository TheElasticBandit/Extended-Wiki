# Elrond

Elrond Half-Elven is the Lord of Rivendell, a very powerful and influencial figure in Middle-Earth. 

## Spawning

Elrond spawns only in the Last Homely House.
  
## Behavior

Elrond will attack any player or creature aligned with an evil faction.
He will wander around aimlessly when they are not fighting enemies.
Upon death, a silver coin will float above the ground where he died, signifying that he will respawn here after a short period of time.

## Equipment

Elrond fights enemies with a Rivendell Sword. His lack of armor and range means it is quite for a prepared player to kill him. Even two orc archers at medium range will kill him, their knockback easily countering his slow movement speed.

## Strategy

- There is no need to fight Elrond, but if you do, it is easy to kill him with a longer weapon such as a spear, or with a bow, since he doesn't have a ranged attack.
  
## Lore

Elrond was a skillful warrior in battle and had commanded various Elven armies, including the Last Alliance. He was as effective at leadership and giving counsel. He also had the gift of foresight, through which he could view lands or events far away from Rivendell. He was also a part of the White Council with Saruman, Gandalf, and Galadriel.
