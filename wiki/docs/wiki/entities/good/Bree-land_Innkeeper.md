# Bree-land Innkeeper

The Bree-land Innkeeper is a trader that specializes in inn comforts.

## Spawning

The Bree-land Innkeeper spawns in the inn, found in Bree-land villages.
  
## Behavior

Bree-land Innkeepers will wander around their inn when not selling goods. Upon death, a silver coin will float above the ground where they died, signifying that they will respawn here after a short period of time.

## Equipment

Bree-land Innkeepers fight with any of the following: Iron and bronze daggers and iron, bronze and stone axes.
  
## Trading

Innkeepers will not trade with players if they have negative alignment with Bree-land. Upon trading with them, the player will be bestowed the advancement **"Barliman's Best"**.

Innkeepers sell the goods of their trade. This includes varies foods and drinks.

Bree-land Innkeepers also buy many supplies, such as raw food, mugs, and pipeweed.

## Strategy

- There is no need to fight a Bree-land Innkeeper, but if you do, it is easy to kill them with a longer weapon such as a spear, or with a bow, since they don't have a ranged attack, just watch out for their axes!

## Lore

The Bree-land people were men who wandered over the Misty Mountains but did not continue west like the Edain. They found the area to be pleasant, and settled there for good. They were there before the Númenóreans, after the kingdom of Arnor, and will be forever according to them.
