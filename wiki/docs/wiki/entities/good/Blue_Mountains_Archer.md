# Blue Mountains Archer

Blue Mountains Archers are a common sight across the Blue Mountains. They spawn randomly as well as in Blue Mountains Strongholds and Settlements.

## Behavior

Archers will attack opposing faction npcs as well as players with less than 0 alignment with the [[blue_mountain_faction|Blue Mountains]].

## Equipment

Blue Mountains Archers wear the regular Blue Dwarven armour, giving them the normal 17 points of armour. However they can also spawn without a helmet taking away two points. They use a normal bow against enemies at long range as they do not have a faction bow, and they use a Blue Dwarven dagger at melee range.

## Hiring

They can be hired for 50 coins from [[blue_mountains_commander|Blue Mountains Commanders]] when one's aignment is >=300 with the [[blue_mountain_faction|Blue Mountains]].

## Strategy

- When fighting archers, a shield is extremely useful to block arrows.
- Try and get close enough for them to switch their weapon to a knife, which toggles on at shorter range. If you are at the right distance, they can't damage you with it.

## Lore

The Blue Mountains Archers are a special unit of the Blue Mountains armies, patrolling and guarding their homeland of Ered Luin.
