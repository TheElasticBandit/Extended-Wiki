# Blue Mountains Warrior

Blue Mountain Warriors are a common sight across the Blue Mountains. They spawn randomly as well as in Blue Mountains Strongholds and Settlements.

## Behavior

Warriors will attack opposing faction npcs as well as players with less than 0 alignment with the [[blue_mountain_faction|Blue Mountains]].

## Equipment

Blue Mountains Warriors wear full Blue Dwarven armour giving them 17 points, although they can sometimes spawn without a helmet. They fight enemies with Blue Dwarven swords and spears, as well as a shield.

## Hiring

They can be hired for 40 coins from [[blue_mountains_commander|Blue Mountains Commanders]] when one's aignment is >=250 with the [[blue_mountain_faction|Blue Mountains]].

## Strategy

- Long weapons are generally more effective against melee NPCs, especially dwarves as they have shorter reach.
- If you are fighting larger numbers, try to draw them away one by one, as a large group can overwhelm you with hits very quickly.

## Lore

The Blue Mountains Warriors are the backbone of the Blue Mountains armies, patrolling and guarding their homeland of Ered Luin.
