# Bree-Hobbit

Bree-Hobbits are the co-inhabitants of Bree-land, residing mainly in Staddle and in Bree itself.

## Spawning

Bree-Hobbits can spawn any time of the day. They are only found in Bree-land.
  
## Behavior

Hobbits are passive mobs. They will flee from Orcs, Wargs, or when attacked by other mobs. This behaviour is true of all Bree-Hobbit traders. Upon killing a Bree-Hobbit, you will be bestowed the advancement **"Bree-hobbit Slayer"**
  
## Hiring

They can be hired for 20 coins from a [[bree-land_sheriff|Bree-land Sheriff]] when one's alignment is >=100 with [[bree_faction|Bree-land]].

## Lore

The Bree-hobbits were Harfoots who fled from the growing might of Angmar when they found the fertile land surronding Bree. Some settled there while other continued their journey that would end in the Shire.
