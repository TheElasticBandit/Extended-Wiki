# Bree-land Farmer

The Bree-land Farmer is a trader that specializes in crops and related items.

## Spawning

The Bree-land Farmer spawns at the Bree-land Farmer House, found in hamlets and villages beside the fields.
  
## Behavior

Bree-land Farmers will wander around their fields when not interacting. Upon death, a silver coin will float above the ground where they died, signifying that they will respawn here after a short period of time.

## Equipment

Bree-land Farmers fight with any of the following: Iron and bronze daggers and iron, bronze and stone axes.
  
## Trading

Farmer will not trade with players if they have negative alignment with Bree-land.

Farmers sell the goods of their trade. This includes crops and seeds.

Bree-land Farmers also buy many supplies, such as hoes and buckets.

## Strategy

- There is no need to fight a Bree-land Farmer, but if you do, it is easy to kill them with a longer weapon such as a spear, or with a bow, since they don't have a ranged attack, just watch out for their axes!

## Lore

The Bree-land people were men who wandered over the Misty Mountains but did not continue west like the Edain. They found the area to be pleasant, and settled there for good. They were there before the Númenóreans, after the kingdom of Arnor, and will be forever according to them.
