# Bree-land Leatherworker

The Bree-land Leatherworker is a trader that specializes in leather and furs.

## Spawning

The Bree-land Leatherworker spawns in the tannery, found in Bree-land villages.
  
## Behavior

Bree-land Leatherworkers will wander around their tannery when not selling goods. Upon death, a silver coin will float above the ground where they died, signifying that they will respawn here after a short period of time.

## Equipment

Bree-land Leatherworkers fight with any of the following: Iron and bronze daggers and iron, bronze and stone axes.
  
## Trading

Leatherworkers will not trade with players if they have negative alignment with Bree-land. Upon trading with them, the player will be bestowed the advancement **"Insider Bargains"**.

Leatherworkers sell the goods of their trade. This leather and leather armor.

Bree-land Leatherworkers also buy many supplies, such as furs.

## Strategy

- There is no need to fight a Bree-land Leatherworker, but if you do, it is easy to kill them with a longer weapon such as a spear, or with a bow, since they don't have a ranged attack, just watch out for their axes!

## Lore

The Bree-land people were men who wandered over the Misty Mountains but did not continue west like the Edain. They found the area to be pleasant, and settled there for good. They were there before the Númenóreans, after the kingdom of Arnor, and will be forever according to them.
