# Bree-land Lumberman

The Bree-land Lumberman is a trader that specializes in logs and leaves.

## Spawning

The Bree-land Lumberman spawns in the lumberyard, found in Bree-land villages.
  
## Behavior

Bree-land Lumbermen will wander around their yard when not selling goods. Upon death, a silver coin will float above the ground where they died, signifying that they will respawn here after a short period of time.

## Equipment

Bree-land Lumbermen fight with a iron axe.
  
## Trading

Lumbermen will not trade with players if they have negative alignment with Bree-land. Upon trading with them, the player will be bestowed the advancement **"Insider Bargains"**.

Lumbermen sell the goods of their trade. This includes logs and beams of various wood types.

Bree-land Lumbermen also buy many supplies, such as axes and shears.

## Strategy

- There is no need to fight a Bree-land Lumberman, but if you do, it is easy to kill them with a longer weapon such as a spear, or with a bow, since they don't have a ranged attack, just watch out for their axes!

## Lore

The Bree-land people were men who wandered over the Misty Mountains but did not continue west like the Edain. They found the area to be pleasant, and settled there for good. They were there before the Númenóreans, after the kingdom of Arnor, and will be forever according to them.
