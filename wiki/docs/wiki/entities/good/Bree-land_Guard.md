# Bree-land Guard

Bree-land Guards are the men tasked with the protection of Bree-land, the area surrounding the four towns of Bree, Archet, Staddle, and Combe.

## Spawning

Bree-land Guards can spawn any time of the day. They only spawn in Bree-land and prevent orcs from appearing.
  
## Behavior

Guards will attack any player or creature aligned with an evil faction, which due to their location, generally means [[gundabad_faction|Gundabad]] units.
They generally wander around aimlessly when they are not fighting enemies.

## Equipment

Bree-land Guards fight with an iron sword and wear a mix of leather and chainmail armour with an iron helmet.
  
## Hiring

They can be hired for 40 coins from a [[bree-land_sheriff|Bree-land Sheriff]] when one's alignment is >=250 with [[bree_faction|Bree-land]].

## Strategy

- When fighting Bree-land Guards, a long-ranged weapon is useful, since they only use swords.
- If you are fighting larger numbers, try to draw them towards you one by one, as a large group can overwhelm you very quickly.

## Lore

The Bree-land people were men who wandered over the Misty Mountains but did not continue west like the Edain. They found the area to be pleasant, and settled there for good. They were there before the Númenóreans, after the kingdom of Arnor, and will be forever according to them.
