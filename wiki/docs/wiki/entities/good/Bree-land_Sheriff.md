# Bree-land Sheriff

The Bree-land Sheriff is the hiring NPC of the people of Bree-land, including the Bree-Hobbits.

## Spawning

The Bree-land Sheriff spawns inside a Bree-land Guardhouse, a small round building with a secondary tent, found in Bree Hamlets.
  
## Behavior

Like other hiring NPCs, the Bree-land Sheriff will not attack any player or evil NPC unless they are attacked first.

## Equipment

Bree-land Sheriffs fight with an iron sword and wear leather armour with an leather hat instead of cap.
  
## Hiring

Bree-land Sheriffs allow you to hire Bree-hobbits, Bree-Men, or Bree-land Guards as long as you have at least 100 alignment and some money. Upon hiring a unit, the player will be bestowed the advancement **"The Greenway Watch"**.

## Strategy

- There is no need to fight a Bree-land Sheriff, but if you do, it is easy to kill them with a longer weapon such as a spear, or with a bow, since they don't have a ranged attack.

## Lore

The Bree-land people were men who wandered over the Misty Mountains but did not continue west like the Edain. They found the area to be pleasant, and settled there for good. They were there before the Númenóreans, after the kingdom of Arnor, and will be forever according to them.
