# Oddment Collector

The Oddment Collector is a traveling trader that spawns near the player and has various goods for sale.

## Spawning

The Oddment Collector will spawn fairly close to a player with a message like "Lunn the Oddment Collector has arrived nearby".
  
## Behavior

The Oddment Collector will not attack any player or creature until they are attacked. They wander around the area they appeared when not be traded with. After awhile, they will despawn.

## Equipment

The Oddment Collector fight enemies with a iron or bronze Dagger. They have no armor, making them weak combat units.
  
## Trading

The Oddment Collector will always trade with players unless they are they have been attacked. Upon trading with then, the player will be bestowed the advancement **"One Man's Trash"**.

Oddment Collectors sell random goods. This includes slime, bamboo, suspicious meat and Shire heather.

Oddment Collectors also buy many supplies, such as gemstones and random items.

## Strategy

- There is no need to fight a Oddment Collector, but if you do, it is easy to kill them with a longer weapon such as a spear, or with a bow, since they don't have a ranged attack.
