# Uruk

Uruks are a tougher breed of Orcs that inhabit the biome of Nan Curunír and are the soldiers of Saruman.

## Spawning

Uruks spawn all over Nan Curunír in droves, as well as the surrounding areas quite regularily, including spawning in Rohan at night.

## Behaviour

Uruks will wander aimlessly around and attack enemy players and NPCs immediately upon recognition. They are much stronger and tougher than other orcs, which makes them harder to kill especially in large numbers.

## Equipment

Uruks wear full Uruk armour which gives them 17 points, equal to Blue Mountains armour and many others. They wield a random weapon, which may include a cleaver, dagger, or other.

## Hiring

Uruks can be hired from [[uruk_chieftain|Uruk Chieftains]] for ## silver coins. Your alignment must be at least ###+ with Isengard to hire them.

## Strategy 

- As they are the same height as you, long ranged weapons like spears are less effective as on normal orcs but still advised when fighting any NPC, as you can still fairly easily keep them out of reach.
- Try to draw them away and fight them one at a time, because many Uruks can overwhelm you, especially with insufficient equipment.

## Lore

Uruks of Isengard were bred by Saruman nearing the end of the Third Age and made up the main part of Saruman's army.
