# Isengard

**Dunland** are the wild men who dwell east of Isengard, and are allied with Saruman.

## NPCs
- [[dunlanding|Dunlendings]] spawn randomly around the Isengard biome and its structures, and is the basic NPC.
- [[dunlanding_warrior|Dunlending Warrior]] spawn randomly around the Isengard biome and its structures
- [[dunlanding_warlord|Dunlending Warlord]] spawn in certain structures in the Isengard biome.

## History
- 1.10.0:
    - Ported Dunlanding Warlord
- Renewed 4.2:
    - Ported Dunlanding
    - Ported Dunlanding Warrior

## Trivia

- Dunland was the third evil faction added to the game after [[gundabad_faction|Gundabad]] and [[isengard_faction|Isengard]]
