# Isengard

**Isengard** is the hordes of orcs created by Saruman, who dwell in Nan Curunír and the surrounding area.

## NPCs
- [[uruk|Uruks]] spawn randomly around the Isengard biome and its structures, and is the basic NPC.
- [[uruk_archer|Uruk Archers]] spawn randomly around the Isengard biome and its structures.
- [[snaga|Snagas]] spawn randomly around the Isengard biome and its structures.
- [[snaga_archer|Snaga Archers]] spawn randomly around the Isengard biome and its structures.
- [[uruk_berserker|Uruk Berserkers]] spawn randomly around the Isengard biome and its structures.
- [[uruk_chieftains|Uruk Captains]] spawn in certain structures in the Isengard biome.
- [[uruk_smith|Uruk Smiths]] spawn in Isengard Smithies.
- [[uruk_suppliers|Uruk Suppliers]] spawn randomly around the Isengard biome and its structures.
- [[warg|Isengard_Wargs]] spawn randomly around the Isengard biome and its structures.
