# Cheese Curds

## Overview

Cheese curds are a pitiful food source and an intermitted step of the cheese production process.

## Obtaining

Right clicking a [[bucket_of_cheese_curds|Bucket of Cheese Curds]] in the players main hand will result in the cheese curds separating from the bucket. 

## Uses

Used to craft [[cheese_wheel|Cheese Wheels]]
{{crafting:cheese_wheel}}

## History

- 1.4.0:
    - Fixed Cheese Curds not having an item texture
- 1.3.0:
    - Added Cheese Curds

## Trivia

Prior to [[version#beta-1-7-4|1.7.4]], cooking [Milk Bucket](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Milk_Bucket) in a furnace would directly result in cheese curds, consuming the bucket and making this an expensive item. With the introduction of the [[bucket_of_cheese_curds|Bucket of Cheese Curds]] item, cheese curds now must be separated from the bucket.
