# Rivendell Equipment

Rivendell Equipment is used by the Elves of Rivendell. It is the most durable and strong Elven equipment (Tied with [[lindon_equipment|Lindon Equipment]]), making it very useful for good players that haven't allied themselves with the dwarves.

## Rivendell Sword

The Rivendell Sword does 7 damage, has 700 durability, and 1.6 Attack Speed. It is one of the best swords in the mod. For comparison, the vanilla iron sword does 6 damage, and has 250 durability.

## Rivendell Dagger

The Rivendell Dagger does 4 damage, has 700 durability, and 2.4 Attack Speed. Although the range is less, its faster swing means a maxed-out dagger has higher damage per second then a maxed-out sword of the same type.

## Rivendell Spear

The Rivendell Spear does 6 damage, has 700 durability, and a 1.33 Attack Speed. Throwing the spear raises its damage to 20, although it does fall off quite quickly.

## Rivendell Axe

The Rivendell Axe does 10 damage, has 700 durability and a 0.9 Attack Speed. 

## Rivendell Pickaxe

The Rivendell Pickaxe does 5 damage and has 700 durability. It mines a little faster than an iron pickaxe, which combined with its durability, makes this a solid choice for mining.

## Rivendell Shovel

The Rivendell Shovel does 5.5 damage, has 700 durability, and is slightly faster than an iron shovel.

## Rivendell Hoe

The Rivendell Hoe does 1 damage and has 700 durability.

## Rivendell Bow

The Rivendell Bow does 8-13 damage, has 1050 durability, and 125% Draw Speed, Range Damage, and Range.
