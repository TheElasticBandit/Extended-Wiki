# Bucket of Cheese Curds

## Overview

The Bucket of [[cheese_curds|Cheese Curds]] is an intermediate item in the cheese production process.

## Obtaining

Obtained by cooking a [Milk Bucket](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Milk_Bucket). Right clicking the resulting Bucket of Cheese Curds in the players main hand will result in the [[cheese_curds|cheese surds]] separating from the bucket. 

## History

- 1.7.4:
    - Added Bucket of Cheese Curds

## Trivia

Prior to [[version#beta-1-7-4|1.7.4]], cooking [Milk Bucket](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Milk_Bucket) in a furnace would directly result in cheese curds, consuming the bucket and making this an expensive item. With the introduction of the Bucket of Cheese Curds item, the bucket can now be reclaimed from the recipe process.
